Library

Libraries are three-tier Central, Institutional and Departmental. Reference books of both Indian and foreign authors, Case study collections, Audio and Video tapes, CDs & DVDs, Databases, Indian and International journals on engineering, computer science & management are available. Aesthetically furnished these libraries are open throughout the day & till late evenings. The library information is computerized/digitalized with facilities for reprography.


Computer Labs

CMS campus is technology driven with Wi-Fi environment. All the departments have state-of-the-art computers with high-speed internet connectivity, projection systems, input-output devices and extensive software support. Students are encouraged to use these for regular curricular work, research, project work, presentations etc.


Multi-Media-Internet Lab

The institute has also developed an exclusive multi-media and internet lab. to offer 24x7 Net Surfing. The lab is equipped with CD Writer, Scanner, Printer, Desktop publishing, Animation Packages, Online Editing, Recording and Video Mixing facilities. All the students have access to internet 24 hours a day through 10 mbps dedicated internet line. The institute has resolved to enhance the internet bandwidth every year.


Sports Center & Gymnasium

CMS takes special care of students' interested in various sports and yoga activities. The institute provides various sports facilities such as Table Tennis, Badminton, Carom, Chess, Basket Ball, Volleyball, Swimming etc.The institute conducts regularly stress management workshops under the supervision of reputed Yoga practitioners to keep the students in high spirits. An impressive gym with fully functional workstation takes care of the passion of the new-gen students towards physical fitness.


Lecture Theaters

Lecture Theaters are air-conditioned and equipped with audio-visual aids and green /white/bulletin boards to provide right kind of ambience to serious studies, deliberations and discussions.


Research

All the departments function with a two fold objectives of delivering useful contents and carry out meaningful research in multi directional fields.

24x7 Helpline : With a view to provide emotional support & establish rapport among teachers, students & parents, the helpline functions under Teacher-Guardian scheme.


Auditorium

CMS also boasts of a centrally air conditioned auditorium with state-of-the-art conferencing and convention facilities with a seating capacity of over 400 persons. All the in- house programs of the group are organized at the auditorium named after the founder secretary Sri Ragendra Swarup. Adjacent to auditorium is located an Art Gallery where exhibitions of the painters regularly take place.


Seminar Hall

The air-conditioned seminar hall has world class presentation & other facilities. The hall is frequently used by leading companies to organize dealers' meet, executive get-togethers, conferences, conventions and even cultural programs.


Language Lab

Language lab is equipped with audio- visual aids, microphone systems and has rich collection of CDs, Video tapes, cassettes on personality developments, interview techniques, vocabulary enhancement, creative writing etc. The air- conditioned lab. offers conducive environment for developing proficiency in English language, a pre- requisite to success in corporate world.

Bus Facility

Inventure Academy has an efficient and reliable transport system with numerous buses and bus stops planned as close to the child’s house as possible.
